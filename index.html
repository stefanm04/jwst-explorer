Witnessing the Cosmos Like Never Before

The James Webb Space Telescope represents humanity’s most ambitious attempt to understand the universe. Orbiting 1.5 million kilometers from Earth, JWST peers deeper into space and time than any observatory before it, unveiling the secrets of the early universe, distant galaxies, and the potential for life beyond our solar system.
